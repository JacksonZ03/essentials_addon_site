Essentials Addon

A collection of quality-of-life features missing in vanilla Blender

Download It Now

Quick Install in 3-Steps

  1. Purchase and download addon from Blender Market.
  2. Drag and drop the ZIP file anywhere inside Blender's UI.
  3. Click "OK" to install.

Gravity Snap

Instantly snap any mesh or empty object to the surface of another mesh object - just as if you dropped it.

Great for quickly aligning stuff in your scene.

  • Works with any mesh or empty object
  • Respects object rotation
  • Maintains object scale

Center Camera Perspective

Quickly center the camera perspective view with a single click.

There's actually no available way to do this in vanilla Blender.

  • Single-click camera centering
  • Works in perspective view
  • Maintains camera settings

Spacebar Play/Pause Toggle

Prevent accidental timeline playback when using spacebar for other applications.

Perfect for following YouTube tutorials or multitasking without accidentally playing your animation.

  • Toggle spacebar animation playback
  • Status indicator in the interface
  • Easily reversible
More features coming soon!

Don't need all the features?

Enable or disable them in the addon preferences.

Out of sight, outta mind!

Change preferences through:

  1. Edit > Preferences > Add-ons
  2. Find "Essentials" in the add-ons list
  3. Expand the addon preferences
